How We Do Things

Personal Approach

At H3 we like to make things personal. Our classes are small, so our expert coaches can motivate everyone individually. They can also help you on your form and give you the confidence to meet your true potential.

Group Excercise

Team work makes the dream work. We believe in supporting each other to get the best results. We also like to be social, be it partner based excercises or working in teams to make the excercise fun. There is no gym hierarchy here, all abilities are welcome.

Nutrition Support

Not sure what macronutrients are. Or how to calculate your calorie intake? Do not worry we can support you with this. When you join, one of our personal trainers will take your measurements and work out the exact amount of calories you need to meet your goals.

Cardio Suite

Designed for those who want to get a sprint in our HIIT classes or prefer to ride alone, We have state of the art Treadmills, Air Bikes, Mountain Bikes and Crosstrainers.

Calisthenics Equipment

Using your own body weight is one of the best ways to measure improvement. In our gym we have Gym Rings, Pull up bars, A rope for climbing. If you need help, one of our expert instructors are on hand to assist.

Free Weights

Do you enjoy doing strength based excercises? Do your workouts consist of Bench presses, Deadlifts and Squats? We have all you will need for that, our dumbbells go up to 60kg. If you think you can handle it we also have a strong man class every Saturday.
